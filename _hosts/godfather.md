Two MIT master's degrees. Engineering and biology from Notre Dame. Decades across heavy manufacturing, defense, energy, broadcasting, higher education, finance, and healthcare. He's seen every circle of hell — and he'll tell you exactly who's running them, in a crisp button-down and suspenders, at a pace that suggests he has somewhere more important to be.

Currently runs self-funded nonprofits rescuing animals and refugees because apparently two advanced degrees and decades of industry experience weren't enough to keep him busy. Suits are Hugo Boss only. Non-negotiable.

Rarely raises his voice. When he does, it lands like a gavel. When he calls you "kiddo" — listen up. He's about to tell you something they don't want you to understand.
